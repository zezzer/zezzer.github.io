Life is a continual journey of growing as a person.

Interests

I've been playing violin for over 15 years.

After self teaching the violin for the 4 years preceding, I joined the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra for violin.

I soloed with the UC Chamber orchestra earlier this year, playing Beethoven's Violin Romance in F.

Back when I attended UC San Diego, I was concertmaster of Intermission Orchestra, a student led ensemble that plays video game and film music.

I enjoy playing chamber music.

I love music because I love learning and improving - when faced with a technique issue, I try to focus on getting down to the root cause of the issue (usually something to do with bow awareness and/or posture). Better technique results in a greater range of expression!

When creating music, my ultimate goal is to play expressively and connect with the audience.
